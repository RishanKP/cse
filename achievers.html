CODE'19 Hackathon Winners

Abhinand and Shilpa of S4 CSE emerged as the winners as India’s largest COVID hackathon, CODE’19, bagging a prize of USD 10,000. CODE’19 was an initiative by the Silicon Valley based Motwani Jadeja Foundation and hosted by HackerRank.
It saw over 6000 participants from all over India and abroad. This achievement by our students is indeed a milestone for our department as well.

100K Installations

An app published by Jaseel of S6 CSE received 1 lakh installations as of April 26, 2020. Going beyond the curriculum and learning new tech and building projects is one thing, but putting it out there and getting such an immense level of acceptance from users all over the world? Inspiring for sure.

The app has no ads as of now and will never have any ads. It has in-app purchases integrated though which will grant you two dark themes
