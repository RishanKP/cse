On 3rd May 2019, the Computer Science association gave a farewell ,titled “End If”, to S8 CSE students. The function was coordinated by S6 CSE students. The venue was GCEK main auditorium. The program began at 2:30 pm.

Head of department Bindu P V, Associate Professor Rafeeq, Assistant Professor Baburaj K V, Assistant Professor Dhanyaja N and Assistant Professor Jobin Joy facilitated the program.

Anagha Asokan,of S4 CSE, who scored cgpa 10 was appreciated.

Over 100 students from third year and fourth year CSE participated. The leaving students shared their various memories and experiences. The program was interspersed with gaming sessions which added colour to the function. Various sfaculty and third year students also shared their well wishes to their seniors.

FOSS CLUB GCEK in association with CSE association conducted free and fair sessions on september 21,2019. The program included a talk on free and open source software and discussion of project ideas. The event began at 10:30am and was concluded by 1:30pm. Almost 30 students attended.The speaker was Mr.Nalin Sathyasheelan, three time winner of google summer of code. his father Mr.Shyasheelan was an honoured guest.

The first thing was to divide the students into groups of 4 or 5. They were asked to discuss among themselves about what they expected from the session, why foss, and how they expected to contribute to the society. Each of the teams presented their insight. Most of them had arrived to know more about foss and Google summer of code.

In his talk, Mr. Nalin explained the importance of foss and shared his experience doing various projects. He also touched upon the subject of cyber security and money making using free software. There was also a flute performance by Mr.Sathyasheelan. At 12, there was tea break for 15 minutes.

After break, Mr. Nalin talked about his various projects on the domain of free software and on making softwares inclusive to blind people. Mr. Sathyasheelan gave various project ideas to the students. Many seemed eager to take up the challenge. There was also a brief glance into google summer of code.

At the end of the session, Abhinav of S5 ME and Vismaya of S5 EC gave their feedback. The program was concluded at 1:30pm with a vote of thanks by Nivanjana of S3 CS.

The computer science and engineering association inaugration of the academic year 2019-20, was held on February 13,2020. The formal ceremony started by 10:30am at the college auditorium.

The chief guest was Mr. Roopak A N,CEO of VoxiLabs,Alumnus of GCEK. The welcome speech was delivered by the association secretary, Arfad Kallil.The Principal-Dr. V O Rejini deliverd the presidential address. Head of Department - Dr.Rafeeque P C and Staff advisor- Prof Rajeev K K gave the felicitation address.

The chief guest inaugrated the function by lighting the lamp, in the prescence of staff and students of comupter science department.

Thejaswi A G, of S6 CSE was honoured for academic excellence- acquiring 10 SGPA in S3. There was also a prize distribution of fantasy premier league conducted by CSE Association.

The chief guest, Roopak A N gave an interactive speech focused on career options for computer science graduates, latest technologies in the field and ethics in technology or tethics. The speech although short was inspiring and impactful. The vote of thanks was delivered by Liyana Sahir of S8 CSE. The ceremony was concluded with Prof.Asjad Nabeel of CSE Department presenting a memento to the chief guest, Roopak A N.

At 12pm there was a colorful procession by the students of Computer Science and Engineering Department from the front gate to the main block.There were also two events conducted in association with inaugration. A treasure hunt was organised by the third year students on the same day. Another event, blind fold was conducted by second years as a pre-event on the day before. Students from all departments participated in the events and won prizes.
